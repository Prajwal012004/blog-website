Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a guest post?

We welcome guest contributions from writers who share our values and can offer valuable insights to our readers. Please send your pitch to submissions@mindfulmusings.com with a brief outline of your proposed topic and some samples of your previous work.

Do you offer advertising or sponsorship opportunities?

Yes, we offer select advertising and sponsorship opportunities that align with our values and would be of interest to our audience. Please contact partnerships@mindfulmusings.com for more information.

How often do you publish new content?

We publish new articles three times a week, typically on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. Subscribe to our newsletter to be notified when new content is available.

Can I republish or reference your content?

You may quote short excerpts from our articles with proper attribution and a link back to the original content. For permission to republish entire articles, please contact us at permissions@mindfulmusings.com.
